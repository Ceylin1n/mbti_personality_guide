Introduction Of Sentinels

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the INFP type include idealistic, creative, sensitive, and value-oriented individuals. They possess a rich inner world.Strengths:Strong imagination, deep empathy, and creativity.Weaknesses:Being overly idealistic, indecisiveness, and sensitivity to criticism.Suitable Professions:Writer, illustrator, consultant, psychologist, social worker, and artist.Famous Examples:Sezen Aksu, Zeynep Bastık, Halil Sezai, Johnny Depp, Florence Welch, Timothée Chalamet.

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the ENFP type include creative, energetic, passionate individuals who inspire others.Strengths:Creativity and originality, strong social communication skills, and ability to motivate.Weaknesses:Scattered focus, indecisiveness, and excessive idealism.Suitable Professions:Advertising, media, arts, psychology, coaching, and entrepreneurship.Famous Examples:Cem Adrian, Ezgi Mola, Hazal Kaya, Robin Williams, Will Smith, Robert Downey Jr.

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the ENFJ type include inspiring, strongly leader-oriented, empathetic, and motivating individuals.Strengths:Strong leadership, empathy, and effective communication.Weaknesses:Taking on excessive responsibility, over-idealism, and indecisiveness.Suitable Professions:Teaching, coaching, leadership, public relations, and psychology.Famous Examples:Halit Ergenç, Ceyda Düvenci, İbrahim Kutluay, Barack Obama, Oprah Winfrey, Emma Stone.

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the INFJ type include deep-thinking, idealistic, visionary individuals who inspire others. They have a strong spiritual side.Strengths:Strong intuition and analysis, empathy and guidance skills, and the ability to see the big picture.Weaknesses:Being overly idealistic, emotional burnout, and social withdrawal.Suitable Professions:Psychologist, writer, therapist, consultant, artist, and social entrepreneur.Famous Examples:Tuba Büyüküstün, Haluk Bilginer, Melisa Sözen, Carl Jung, J.K. Rowling, Nicole Kidman.
