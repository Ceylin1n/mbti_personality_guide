General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the ENTP type include idea-generating, innovative, debate-loving, and curious individuals.Strengths:Their strengths are quick thinking, creative problem-solving, and mental agility.Weaknesses:Their weaknesses are excessive debating, difficulty in completing projects, and lack of focus.Suitable Professions:Suitable professions include entrepreneurship, advertising, management consulting, law, and innovation.Famous Examples:Okan Bayülgen, Ata Demirer, Ceyhun Yılmaz, Steve Jobs, Tom Hanks, Sacha Baron Cohen.

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the ENTJ type include decisive, visionary, strategically thinking, and strong leader personalities.Strengths:Effective management, vision and planning, and resilience to challenges.Weaknesses:Emotional distance, dominant attitude, and impatience.Suitable Professions:CEO, manager, entrepreneur, legal expert, and organizational leader.Famous Examples:Bülent Ecevit, Güler Sabancı, Esra Erol, Margaret Thatcher, Julius Caesar, Harrison Ford.

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the INTJ type include analytical, independent, and strategically thinking individuals. They enjoy solving complex problems.Strengths:Visionary thinking, solving difficult problems, discipline, and independence.Weaknesses:Impatience, difficulty in emotional expression, and an overly critical approach.Suitable Professions:Scientist, engineer, data analyst, strategy consultant, and software developer.Famous Examples:İlber Ortaylı, Cem Yılmaz, Gaye Su Akyol, Elon Musk, Christopher Nolan, Mark Zuckerberg.

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the INTP type include individuals who perform deep analysis, think abstractly, and are curious about theories.Strengths:Logical analysis, creative problem-solving, and independence.Weaknesses:Difficulty in practical application, appearing socially distant, and being overly critical.Suitable Professions:Academician, software developer, engineer, researcher, and mathematician.Famous Examples:Can Gürzap, Ozan Güven, Teoman, Albert Einstein, Bill Gates, Stephen Hawking.
