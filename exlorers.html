Introduction Of Sentinels

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the ISTP type include practical, logical, solution-oriented individuals who are talented in technical fields. They are calm yet curious.Strengths:Composure during crises, technical talent, and independent working.Weaknesses:Reluctance toward long-term planning, emotional detachment, and distance from authority.Suitable Professions:Engineering, piloting, repair/technical work, policing, and software development.Famous Examples:Engin Altan Düzyatan, Nejat İşler, Tardu Flordun, Clint Eastwood, Tom Cruise, Bear Grylls.

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the ESFP type include joyful, social, energetic individuals who love to shine among people.Strengths:Charisma, adaptability, and positivity.Weaknesses:Difficulty in planning, impulsive decisions, and getting bored quickly.Suitable Professions:Acting, music, performing arts, fashion, and public relations.Famous Examples:Gülşen, Aleyna Tilki, Oğuzhan Koç, Marilyn Monroe, Justin Bieber, Kylie Minogue.

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the ISFP type include quiet, sensitive, emotionally deep individuals with strong artistic inclinations.Strengths:Creativity, aesthetic sensitivity, and adaptability.Weaknesses:Difficulty in decision-making, excessive sensitivity, and difficulty in working systematically.Suitable Professions:Art, design, music, fashion, photography, and therapy.Famous Examples:Mabel Matiz, Serenay Sarıkaya, Beren Saat, Michael Jackson, Lana Del Rey, Brad Pitt.

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the ESTP type include energetic, social, practical individuals who are eager to take risks.Strengths:Rapid decision-making, effectiveness in demanding environments, and social charm.Weaknesses:Impatience, lack of focus, and taking excessive risks.Suitable Professions:Sales, entrepreneurship, sports, emergency response, and commerce.Famous Examples:Acun Ilıcalı, Demet Akalın, Murat Boz, Madonna, Ernest Hemingway, Samuel L. Jackson.
