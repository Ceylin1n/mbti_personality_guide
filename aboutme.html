Introduction Of Sentinels

Hello, I am Ceylin KALE. I have a profound interest in understanding human behavior, personality structures, and learning processes. This website is a project that has emerged from my extensive research on the MBTI personality system. My primary objective in creating this site is to present MBTI in a simple, clear, and easily readable manner without overwhelming the reader with complex terminology.Although there is an abundance of content about MBTI on the internet, much of it is either overly superficial or filled with stereotypical descriptions that lack a scientific foundation. For this reason, I wanted to compile the knowledge I acquired through my own research to create a more organized, accurate, and user-friendly resource. Thank you for visiting this website. I hope the information you find here helps you better understand both yourself and the people around you.
