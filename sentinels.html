Introduction Of Sentinels

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the ESTJ type include organized, management-loving, logical, and decisive individuals.Strengths:Their strengths are leadership, organizational skills, and clear communication.Weaknesses:Their weaknesses are inflexibility, difficulty in accepting new perspectives, and being overly critical.Suitable Professions:Project manager, military officer, police officer, financial manager, and administrator.Famous Examples:Bergüzar Korel, Fahriye Evcen, Aras Bulut İynemli, Beyoncé, Kate Middleton, Mother Teresa.

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the ISFJ type include kind, loyal, helpful, and people-oriented individuals.Strengths:Strong empathy skills, a deep sense of responsibility, and great attention to detail.Weaknesses:Neglecting their own needs, difficulty in adapting to major changes, and sensitivity to criticism.Suitable Professions:Nursing, teaching, social work, medical services, and administrative roles.Famous Examples:Selda Bağcan, Barış Özcan, Arzum Onan, Selena Gomez, Prince Charles.

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the ESFJ type include warm-hearted, social, harmonious individuals who enjoy helping people.Strengths:Social harmony, helpfulness, and responsibility.Weaknesses:Seeking approval, excessive self-sacrifice, and resistance to change.Suitable Professions:Teaching, guidance, social work, management, and human resources.Famous Examples:Seda Bakan, Demet Özdemir, Burcu Esmersoy, Jennifer Garner, Hugh Jackman, Taylor Swift.

General Characteristics:The general characteristics of the ISTJ type include organized, responsible, patient individuals who are committed to rules. They enjoy systematic thinking and structured work.Strengths:Being reliable and consistent, skills in establishing and maintaining order, and long-term planning ability.Weaknesses:Resistance to flexibility, difficulty in expressing emotions, and being overly critical.Suitable Professions:Accountant, engineer, legal expert, project manager, auditor, and logistics specialist.Famous Examples:Kıvanç Tatlıtuğ, Cansu Dere, Kenan İmirzalıoğlu, Natalie Portman, Matt Damon, Angela Merkel, Queen Elizabeth II.
